O Allah: You are the Lord of glory and greatness
اللّهُمّ أَهْلَ الْكِبْرِيَاءِ وَالْعَظَمَةِ،
And the Lord of magnanimity and omnipotence
وَأَهْلَ الْجُودِ وَالْجَبَرُوتِ،
And the Lord of pardon and mercy,
وَأَهْلَ الْعَفْوِ وَالرّحْمَةِ،
And the worthiest of being feared and the Lord of forgiveness;
وَأَهْلَ التّقْوَى وَالْمَغْفِرَةِ،
I beseech You in the name of this day,
أَسْأَلُكَ بِحَقّ هذَا الْيَوْمِ
which You have decided to be a feast for the Muslims,
الّذِي جَعَلْتَهُ لِلْمُسْلِمِينَ عِيداً،
And to be safety and increasing honor for Muhammad—
وَلِمُحَمّدٍ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِه
peace of Allah be upon him and his Household—
ذُخْراً وَمَزِيداً،
(I beseech You) that You may send blessings upon Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad,
أَنْ تُصَلّيَ عَلَى مُحَمّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمّدٍ
And You may include me with every item of goodness
وَأَنْ تُدْخِلَنِي فِي كُلّ خَيْرٍ
with which You have included Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad,
أَدْخَلْتَ فِيهِ مُحَمّداً وَآلَ مُحَمّدٍ،
And that You save me from any item of evil
وَأَنْ تُخْرِجَنِي مِنْ كُلِّ سُوءٍ
from which You have saved Muhammad and the Household of Muhammad,
أَخْرَجْتَ مِنْهُ مُحَمّداً وَآلَ مُحَمّدٍ
May Your blessings be upon him and upon them.
صَلَوَاتُكَ عَلَيْهِ وَعَلَيْهِمْ.
O Allah: I pray You for the best of all that
اللّهُمّ إِنّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ مَا
for which Your righteous servants have prayed You,
سَأَلَكَ عِبَادُكَ الصَّالِحُونَ،
And I seek Your protection against all that
وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِمَّا اسْتَعَاذَ مِنْهُ
against which Your sincere servants have asked Your protection.
عِبَادُكَ المُخْلِصُون.
